## Energy demand forecasting through coherent scenarios of production activity of the automotive industry

Repository with code and data for the paper entitled "Energy demand forecasting through coherent scenarios of production activity of the automotive industry" to be published on...

- Hierarchical automotive industry production data is at "producao.csv".
- Energy and aggregated production data are found at "industria_auto.csv".
- Coherent production scenarios obtained through the best reconciliation scenarios are at "fc_filt.csv".
- Production reconciliation modeling and rolling forecasting origin cross-validation is at "Production_autos_reconciliation.qmd" or even at https://robsonpro.github.io/Energy-demand-forecasting-through-coherent-scenarios-of-production-activity-of-the-auto-industry/Production_autos_reconciliation.html.
- Energy modeling and rolling forecasting origin cross-validation, besides forecasting using production scenarios obtained through the best reconciliation scenarios, is at "energia_prod_auto.qmd" or even at  https://robsonpro.github.io/Energy-demand-forecasting-through-coherent-scenarios-of-production-activity-of-the-auto-industry/energia_prod_auto.html.
